About Tutor

Hello my name is Direen. I know that English might not always be easy. I also had problems at the beginning but with practice it got easier. I´m sure that we can improve your English with specific exercises. I find it important to make a learning plan with you that also integrates your topic and exercise wishes. That means that I don´t say which topic we are going to do.

integrate your wishes as best as I can. Some of your wishes could be preparations for an exam, class test or a presentation, reading comprehension, gramma, or a listening comprehension. Please remember that these are only examples your wish could be completely different. If you have a different wish just talk to me or write me and we will look for a way to integrate your wishes into the lesson.
During a lesson we typically do one or two topics. Also important to me is that you are comfortable before, during and after the lessen. I don´t want you to be afraid to ask questions or any other wishes or concerns.

At the moment I am a student at the IU International Hochschule. I´m studying International Management in English. I finished school in July 2020.

From 2011 till 2014 I lived in South Africa and speak English fluently. I also might not always know all answers to a gramma topic but I will most definitely look them up for you.

From March 2019 till June 2020, I tortured a Realschülerin (a secondary school student) in English. I prepared her for her English exam which she passed successfully. A main part of the tutoring was going over topics like USA and Great Britain, Grammar, Writing and translation. Of course, we also talked, and I corrected the pronunciation if necessary. Since November 2020 I have been teaching here on Preply, mainly school students. I mainly prepared my students for their Abitur (an exam) in English. Other students I help with Grammar and their language.

If you are interested in a trial lesson, please contact me.
For me it is incredibly helpful if you write me, why you want to improve your English and how I can help you. You can also write me what you would like to talk about in your trial lesson. During the trial lesson I will ask you more precise on how I can help you, where you need help and what exactly you would like to learn.
